Tips for Resume


Your resume is your first impression. A strong, well-structured resume highlights your skills, experience, and achievements.

  • Keep it clear and concise (1-2 pages max).
  • Highlight skills relevant to the job role.
  • Use action words like "Developed", "Led", "Achieved".
  • Update it regularly as you gain new experience.
